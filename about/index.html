The Founder's Story

Built on Quality & Trust

AV Window Tinting began with a clear mission: to provide the Antelope Valley with window tinting services that prioritize quality, professionalism, and customer satisfaction. We saw too many properties with poor installations and decided to set a higher standard.

We've grown into the region's premier choice for residential, commercial, and automotive window tinting. We understand the High Desert's intense heat and sun, ensuring your windows provide maximum comfort and protection year-round.
